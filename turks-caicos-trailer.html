[Trailer] Turks & Caicos, Worricker Trilogy

13th March 2014
tv

Turks & Caicos is the second in David Hare's Worricker trilogy, featuring Bill Nighy as Johnny Worricker. 

To those of you for whom the above is mostly nonsense, Turks & Caicos is the sequel to Page Eight. The third in the trilogy is also due to broadcast this year.

Turks & Caicos will be on BBC2 at 9pm on Thursday 20th March. It also stars Winona Ryder, Helena Bonham-Carter and Ralph Fiennes.
