[Tweets] True Detective: A Failure in Basic Storytelling

10th March 2014
tv

At some point I will talk more about fathers in True Detective which is an arena that is heavily nodded at but not actually interrogated. It's the flipside of the vilification of mothers, which in turn informs the show"s perspective on women.

But until then: what a shame. I was willing to go with the show"s flaws because I thought there would be some sort of narrative coup. I thought we"d be able to put the episodes end to end and see the syntax clearly. That was not to be. This is a story that cared more about hugging its protagonists than it did delivering its victims. What a shame.
