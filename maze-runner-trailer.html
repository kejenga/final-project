[Trailer] The Maze Runner

[Trailer] The Maze Runner

18th March 2014
film

Even though I hated the books, I suspect I'm going to get a kick out of The Maze Runner. There's not a lot that can be done to the plot - in which a group of teenage boys are stuck in the middle of a maze and don't know why - but the first of the four books was easily the most compelling, and translation to film means we won't have to slog through that painful first-person POV.

Plus, it's a great cast for a teen flick, headed up by Teen Wolf favourite Dylan O'Brien, Skins actress Kaya Scodelario, and this year's EE BAFTA Rising Star, Will Poulter. 

Here's MTV's first trailer. The film is due out in cinemas some time in the Autumn.
