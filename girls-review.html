Girls, 3x07, Beach House

Girls, 3x07, Beach House

10th March 2014
tv

The below post contains spoilers for this week's Girls

I loved this week's Girls. Not only did I find it hysterically funny - Danny Strong plays part-creep, part-smarm so well, and Hannah and Marnie's way of managing to speak both directly and somehow indirectly about themselves is always a riot ("I am okay," Marnie says. "I may not seem okay, and I may not be okay now, but I am, like, okay.") - but I love any episode where people are put together in order to rouse conflict.

Stunningly, at the episode's centre, is Shoshanna's angry-drunk meltdown. Shoshanna has always been the best of the characters for me. To begin with she was the most overtly archetypal; she was far more a character than she was a full person. But over the show's three seasons, not only has she made the most growth, but she's been the most direct and on point. I would go as far as suggesting that Shoshanna is the audience's entry into the girls' lives because despite her agitated rambling, she is focused, systematic, and cutting. Shoshanna doesn't aim to wound but she knows how to cut through bullshit, and in a season that is ever-increasingly about balancing egocentricity with self-effacement,

Of course, also spectacular was Marnie's failure of a dinner - really? She wanted to have that "healing" catharsis over dinner? - and the synchronised dance. Nothing made me laugh quite as much as seeing the four girls emerge from behind the windowed doors, or that half-baked recreation at the end of the episode. Despite Marnie's plans falling apart, the four friends do manage to air their grievances. the next step is probably a return to looking inwards, but for a little while everything was out in the open.
